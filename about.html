Debone Photos and Video Editing Studio is a Port Harcourt company built on quality product and honest service — for every customer, every time.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2006, still developing.

What started as a small photo and video outfit in Port Harcourt has grown into a full studio — covering events, training the next generation of editors, and keeping the city's computers and printers running.

We're a company that focuses on offering quality products, goods and services to people in any part of the world we can reach — starting right here in Rivers State.
